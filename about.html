Research.




I start my research with : An overview of the company. I study all the work that is perviously done, the style gudes and work-flow of the company. Then I study about the company's needs. I like to research about the target audience's needs. I study about the target audience's type(primary-secondary), age-group,location, language, any barriers (if there are any). I learn about any competitors in the same field. I get reviews from people about the strengths and weaknesses in the current model(like website, blog, logo, brochure, videos, photos anything related to the field of work i am going to do)of the company. And then I move forward to the Work Phase.







Work.




I start my work with : Building the new product that is required by the company according to the customer's needs. Building process begins keeping in mind all the research done in the earlier stage. The process is an itterative process. After achieving the results according to the designed prototype or thumbnail. The customer reviews the product and gives their feedback and sugestions about any changes if necessary. If any changes are require then the product goes in an iterative phase of recreating and getting approval.







Deliver.




I start my delivery with : After the product is approved by the customer, the product goes for a dummy launch and getting feedback from people for user experience testing. The reviews help me to understand the flaws and the pot-holes in the product and help me to enhance my final product. This leads to a renewed design and changes to the product according to the target audience's needs. After all the work is done there is again a review and user experience testing of the product and then the final delivery of the product. Thsi basically leade to a sucessefull final launch.
